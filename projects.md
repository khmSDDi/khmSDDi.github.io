## Research Projects

* **2022-2026 | PhD Researcher**
  ### [RiverWatch: a citizen science approach to river pollution monitoring](https://sites.google.com/view/riverwatch/home-page){:target="_blank"}
  A transdisciplinary project that aims to advance river monitoring in Sarno River specific to pollution events, with a special focus on macroplastics, utilizing optical camera systems and citizen science. This project involves a consortium of four Italian universities, government agencies, and civic society.
